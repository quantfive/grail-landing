what we Offer

Quick and easy testing

Test your app

Grail offers an quick and effective way to test out your application. We replace the need for you to manually walk through your entire application.

What it does

Grail runs through each page of your application and tests the functionality on every page. Currently works for all functionality that triggers with a click.

In Progress

Grail is actively being worked on and is improving day by day. Support for more event handling and functionality will be released when complete.
